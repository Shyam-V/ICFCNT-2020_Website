Important Dates


  Publication Policy  

  • Any manuscript submitted to ICFCNT-2020 must be original and the content thereof, or substantial parts of it, must not be under consideration / published by any other journal.
  • All the presented papers to ICFCNT-2020 will be published in “Scopus Indexed Journals”.
  • Minimum length of the paper is 4 pages and maximum length of the paper is 10 pages.
  • All submissions to ICFCNT-2020 are assessed by the chairs of the respective Tracks, who will decide whether they are suitable for peer review based on:
    • The similarity index of a professional plagiarism checker, which should be less than 15%,
    • The timeliness, the interest and importance of the topic.
  • The peer review process involves the review of the manuscripts by two independent reviewers.
  • Track chairs will make a decision on the acceptance of the manuscript for publication based on the reviewers’ reports and the reports will be sent to the authors for suitable modification.
  • Publishing in journals will take a minimum of 3 months to 6 months from the date of the conference.
  • Only the papers that are presented in the conference will be considered for publication.
